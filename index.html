The Problem

Your engineers are spending skilled hours on paperwork.

Companies like RCG USA run lean technical teams across commissioning, dry dock, and on-board interventions. If a technician spends just 1–2 hours writing up each job, and your team handles 10 jobs a week, that's 10–20 hours of skilled engineering time lost to paperwork — every week.

AI can handle that layer — instantly, consistently, and without errors. These aren't hypothetical tools. Every demo below is live and built specifically with RCG's workflows in mind.

  • Field Report Writing Technicians spend 1–2 hours per job on write-ups that follow the same structure every time — up to $100/hr in billable time per report.
  • Manual Lookup On Board Engineers search hundreds of pages of documentation for answers they need in minutes, not hours.
  • Fault Diagnosis Under Pressure Diagnosing drive, motor, and generator faults often relies entirely on individual engineer experience.
  • Proposal & Quote Generation Senior engineers write proposals by hand — time that could be spent on actual engineering decisions.
